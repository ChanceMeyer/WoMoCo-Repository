In this fast-paced world, the professional mother has many hats to wear mom, provider, employee, leader, and teacher to name just a few. How do they keep it all together, how do they strike a balance with the demands of their professional and personal lives?

With the Working Mothers Connection (WoMoCo) the professional mother can conne about.htmlct with other professional moms and share their experiences, give tips, network or just find out where they can get their hair done or a good mani-pedi!
